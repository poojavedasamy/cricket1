matheesha pathirana

Matheesha Pathirana (Sinhala: මතීෂ පතිරණ; Tamil: மதிஷ பத்திரன; born 18 December 2002) is a professional Sri Lankan cricketer who currently plays limited overs cricket for the national team.[1] He has been referred to as Baby Malinga, as he has modelled his bowling action after Lasith Malinga in sling action.[2
